About Us

A Think Tank Defining The Future of Reality through developing a New Roadmap for Humanity. We are a non for profit future-driven global think tank inspired by the international bestseller The New Digital World Order. At the Web 3 Institute, we believe that Web 3 will revolutionize how we interact with each other and with technology. We are committed to helping governments and businesses understand this new technology and its implications for our lives, businesses, and society as a whole. Our members are all digital disrupters from various industries who have led digital transformation for global fortune 500 companies, G20 governments, and globally top-ranked academic institutions.
